About Us

Nature Trails is a renowned outdoor adventure company that specializes in providing thrilling nature experiences to adventure enthusiasts. We are dedicated to exploring the beauty of nature and creating unforgettable memories.

Established in 2013, Nature Trails has been at the forefront of promoting sustainable tourism and ecotourism practices. Our team of experienced guides and naturalists ensures that each trip is not only exciting but also educational, allowing our guests to learn about the local flora, fauna, and ecosystems.

Our head office is located in Nairobi, Kenya, serving as the central hub for coordinating our nature trails, expeditions, and eco-tours across various breathtaking destinations. We strive to offer exceptional customer service and prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our clients.
